Story

Part 1 · A Dream in the Tropics

Leo was a young entrepreneur/ˌɒntrəprəˈnɜːr/ who dreamed of changing people's lives through education. Unlike many business owners who focused only on profits, he believed that knowledge could transform entire communities. One day, he decided to establish a learning centre in the tropics/ˈtrɒpɪks/, a region known for its rich natural resources but limited educational opportunities.

Leo 是一位年轻的 企业家(entrepreneur)，他梦想通过教育改变人们的生活。与许多只关注利润的商人不同，他相信知识能够改变整个社区。一天，他决定在 热带地区(tropics) 建立一所学习中心。那里自然资源丰富，但教育机会十分有限。

The land he selected had remained vacant/ˈveɪkənt/ for years. Several abandoned buildings stood silently among the trees. Many local residents had already left in search of better opportunities elsewhere. Looking at the empty valley, Leo saw not a problem but a possibility.

他选择的土地已经 空置的(vacant) 很多年了。几栋废弃建筑静静地矗立在树林之间。许多当地居民早已离开这里，到别处寻找更好的机会。而 Leo 在这片空旷的山谷中看到的不是问题，而是希望。

To begin the project, he invested most of his personal capital/ˈkæpɪtl/. Friends warned him about the risks, but he was determined. He chose to devote/dɪˈvəʊt/ himself completely to the academy and promised not to abandon it halfway.

为了启动项目，他投入了自己大部分的 资本(capital)。朋友们警告他这样做风险很大，但他态度坚定。他决定将自己完全 奉献于(devote) 这所学院，并承诺绝不会半途而废。

However, not everyone supported his vision. Some people speculated/ˈspekjuleɪt/ that the academy would fail. Others believed that the region's poor transportation and serious traffic congestion/kənˈdʒestʃən/ would prevent students from attending classes regularly.

然而，并不是所有人都支持他的愿景。有些人 推测(speculate) 学院最终会失败。还有人认为当地落后的交通条件以及严重的 交通拥堵(congestion) 会导致学生无法正常上课。

Some critics even argued that the project might aggravate/ˈæɡrəveɪt/ existing economic problems. They worried that valuable resources would be wasted. Yet Leo decided to persist/pəˈsɪst/ despite all the doubts.

一些批评者甚至认为这个项目可能会 加剧(aggravate) 当地已有的经济问题。他们担心宝贵的资源会被浪费。然而，面对所有质疑，Leo 依然选择 坚持(persist) 下去。

At the beginning, there was considerable ambiguity/ˌæmbɪˈɡjuːəti/ regarding the school's future direction. Should it focus on science? Agriculture? Business? Every proposal seemed reasonable.

项目初期，学校未来的发展方向存在很大的 模糊性(ambiguity)。它应该重点发展科学教育？农业教育？还是商业课程？每一种方案似乎都有道理。

Several experts suggested an alternative/ɔːlˈtɜːnətɪv/ approach. Instead of creating a school, they proposed building a tourist resort. The debate continued for months.

一些专家提出了 替代性的(alternative) 方案。他们建议不要建学校，而是修建旅游度假村。双方争论持续了好几个月。

Finally, after numerous meetings, a broad consensus/kənˈsensəs/ emerged. The academy would combine education, environmental protection, and vocational training. The goal became clear and explicit/ɪkˈsplɪsɪt/.

最终，在无数次会议之后，大家达成了广泛的 共识(consensus)。学院将结合教育、环境保护和职业培训。项目目标也变得十分 明确的(explicit)。

Leo summarized the project's central notion/ˈnəʊʃən/ in a single sentence: "Education should help people improve their lives while protecting the environment."

Leo 用一句话总结了项目的核心 观念(notion)： “教育应该帮助人们改善生活，同时保护环境。”

To secure government support, the academy had to meet a strict deadline/ˈdedlaɪn/. If construction was not completed within eighteen months, funding would be withdrawn.

为了获得政府支持，学院必须满足严格的 截止期限(deadline)。如果工程不能在十八个月内完成，资金支持就会被取消。

Part 2 · The First Students

When construction finally began, Leo realized that buildings alone could not create a successful school. The most important task was helping young people develop confidence and ambition. The academy's first students were mainly juveniles/ˈdʒuːvənaɪlz/ from nearby villages. Some were only twelve or thirteen years old. Others were older adolescents/ˌædəˈlesnts/ preparing for higher education. Leo believed that education should not merely transfer knowledge. Teachers needed to instil/ɪnˈstɪl/ positive values and encourage students to think independently.

Leo 明白，仅仅拥有教学楼并不能成就一所优秀学校。学院第一批学生大多是来自附近村庄的 青少年(juveniles)。其中一些只有十二三岁，而另一些则是即将进入大学阶段的 青少年(adolescents)。 他认为教育不仅仅是传授知识，更重要的是向学生 灌输(instil) 正确的价值观，并培养独立思考能力。

To keep students engaged, the teachers worked hard to motivate/ˈməʊtɪveɪt/ them. Instead of relying entirely on textbooks, they organized projects, experiments, and outdoor activities. One language teacher introduced the concept of collocation/ˌkɒləˈkeɪʃən/. She explained that native speakers naturally combine certain words together. Understanding these combinations would help students sound more natural when speaking English.

为了激发学生的学习热情，老师们努力 激励(motivate) 他们。课堂不再局限于课本，而是增加了项目实践、实验和户外活动。 其中一位英语老师专门讲解了 搭配(collocation) 的概念。她告诉学生，英语母语者会自然地把某些词放在一起使用，掌握这些搭配能够让表达更加地道。

The school quickly became popular. Parents appreciated its practical teaching methods. Students enjoyed learning through real-life experiences. More importantly, they began to believe that education could genuinely change their future.

学校很快受到欢迎。 家长们欣赏这种注重实践的教学方式，学生们也喜欢这种与现实生活紧密结合的学习体验。更重要的是，他们开始相信教育真的能够改变未来。

Part 3 · Ecology and Responsibility

Environmental education became one of the academy's most important subjects. The surrounding region possessed a unique ecology/iːˈkɒlədʒi/. Dense forests, rivers, and diverse wildlife formed a delicate natural system. Scientists warned that careless development could have serious adverse/ˈædvɜːs/ consequences.

环境教育逐渐成为学院最重要的课程之一。 当地拥有独特的 生态系统(ecology)。茂密的森林、河流以及丰富的野生动物共同构成了脆弱而复杂的自然体系。科学家警告说，不合理开发可能带来严重的 不利的(adverse) 后果。

During a scientific excursion/ɪkˈskɜːʃən/, students explored nearby forests and wetlands. They observed insects creeping/kriːpɪŋ/ through leaves and documented local species. One group studied agricultural commodities/kəˈmɒdətiz/ produced in the region. They discovered how local farmers depended on international markets and how environmental changes could affect economic development.

在一次科学 远足(excursion) 中，学生们深入森林和湿地考察。他们观察昆虫缓慢 爬行(creep) 的过程，并记录当地物种。 另一组学生研究了当地生产的农业 商品(commodities)。他们了解到农民如何依赖国际市场，以及生态变化如何影响经济发展。

The students collected soil samples. Some contained large amounts of clay/kleɪ/. Others were rich in organic materials. They also discovered insects marked with unusual stripes/straɪps/, which later became the subject of several research projects.

学生们采集了大量土壤样本。 有些样本富含 黏土(clay)，有些则含有丰富的有机物。他们还发现了一些拥有特殊 条纹(stripes) 的昆虫，这些发现后来成为研究课题。

Part 4 · Growth and Recognition

As the academy expanded, the government provided additional provisions/prəˈvɪʒnz/ for education and research. Several companies agreed to upgrade/ʌpˈɡreɪd/ laboratories, libraries, and computer facilities. These improvements greatly enhanced the quality of learning.

随着学院不断发展，政府提供了更多的 补给与支持(provisions)。 与此同时，多家公司出资 升级(upgrade) 实验室、图书馆以及计算机设施，大幅提高了教学质量。

A national newspaper later investigated the academy's achievements. Reporters carefully examined the veracity/vəˈræsəti/ of its success stories. Fortunately, the evidence was strong. Many local residents shared personal anecdotes/ˈænɪkdəʊts/ describing how the academy had transformed their lives.

后来，全国性报纸开始调查学院的办学成果。 记者们认真核实这些成功案例的 真实性(veracity)。幸运的是，证据十分充分。 许多居民分享了个人 轶事(anecdotes)，讲述学院如何改变了他们的人生。

One particularly moving story came from a former student whose family had lived in poverty for generations. His success generated widespread sympathy/ˈsɪmpəθi/ and admiration. During the annual celebration, a local symphony/ˈsɪmfəni/ orchestra performed for the community. The music stirred powerful emotions/ɪˈməʊʃnz/ among the audience.

其中最感人的故事来自一位贫困家庭出身的毕业生。 他的经历引发了广泛的 同情(sympathy) 和敬佩。在周年庆典上，当地交响乐团演奏了一场精彩的 交响乐(symphony)。 音乐唤起了观众强烈的 情感(emotion)。

Part 5 · The Future

Years later, the once-poor valley became prosperous. Several hotels and conference centres appeared. Some were surprisingly opulent/ˈɒpjələnt/, but Leo always reminded people that wealth should remain a tool rather than a goal.

多年以后，这片曾经贫穷的山谷变得繁荣起来。 酒店和会议中心陆续建成，其中一些甚至十分 豪华的(opulent)。但 Leo 始终提醒大家，财富只是工具，而不应该成为最终目标。

A large cosmetic/kɒzˈmetɪk/ company eventually established a factory nearby. Environmental monitoring remained strict, ensuring that economic growth and ecological protection stayed balanced.

一家大型 化妆品的(cosmetic) 企业后来在附近建立工厂。严格的环境监管确保了经济发展与生态保护之间的平衡。

The current/ˈkʌrənt/ generation benefited enormously from the academy. Every element/ˈelɪmənt/ of the project—from education to environmental protection—worked together to create lasting change.

如今这一 当前的(current) 一代人从学院中获益良多。从教育到环保，项目中的每一个 要素(element) 都共同推动了长期发展。

Researchers around the world requested duplicate/ˈdjuːplɪkət/ copies of reports and teaching materials. International partnerships became increasingly intimate/ˈɪntɪmət/, built on trust and shared values.

世界各地的研究机构纷纷索取报告和教材的 副本(duplicate)。国际合作关系也变得更加 密切的(intimate)，建立在信任和共同价值观之上。

Leo often reflected on the academy's journey. There had been moments when public opinion tilted/tɪltɪd/ against the project. There had also been failures and setbacks. Yet the academy survived because its vision was genuine/ˈdʒenjuɪn/.

Leo 经常回顾学院的发展历程。 曾经有一段时间，公众舆论一度 倾向于(tilt) 反对项目。学院也经历过失败与挫折。 但它最终成功了，因为它的理念是真正 真诚的(genuine)。

Each subsequent/ˈsʌbsɪkwənt/ generation inherited that vision and carried it forward. And that was perhaps the academy's greatest achievement.

每一代 后续的(subsequent) 学生都继承并延续了这一理念。 而这，也许正是这所学院最伟大的成就。
